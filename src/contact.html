We have a low traffic mailing list for discussing the forthcoming events and remembering past ones. Alternatively you can join us in IRC at #lbw on OFTC.

You may be able to find some hikers on PMR446, using channel 4, CTCSS 7.

To let us know that you are coming, please use our shiny registration system. To prevent spam, registrations need to be manually approved, this normally doesn't take more than a couple of days. [% END %]
