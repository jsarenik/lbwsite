LBW Alfriston 2010: Walking

Alfriston sits on the South Downs Way long distance path. There are many footpaths, most of them well-marked, and a good supply of pubs along the way. The local Cuckmere Community Bus runs a service for walkers, taking people to and from the ends of popular walks.
